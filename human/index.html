How It Works

1

Create pzp.yourdomain.com

Set up a subdomain for your PZP site. Keep your human site unchanged.

2

Add manifest.json

Define what your site is, what it offers, and what agents can do.

3

Add context/essential.md

Write ~500 tokens of essential context. Everything an agent needs to start.

4

Add actions/index.json

Define available actions: endpoints, inputs, outputs, errors.

5

Done

Machines can now consume your site efficiently. Your human site stays beautiful.
