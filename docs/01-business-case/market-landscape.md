## Part 1: The Search Transformation

### Traditional Search Is Declining

| Metric | Value | Timeline | Source |
|--------|-------|----------|--------|
| Search engine volume drop | **25%** | By 2026 | Gartner |
| Google searches per user decline | **20%** | 2024-2025 | ALM Corp |
| Zero-click searches | **58-69%** | 2024-2025 | Similarweb |
| AI Overviews coverage | **27.43%** of queries | Nov 2025 | Industry data |

**What this means:** One quarter of all search engine queries will shift to AI platforms within the next year. The majority of remaining searches don't even result in website visits anymore.

### AI Overviews Are Destroying Click-Through Rates

| Metric | Before AI Overviews | After AI Overviews | Change |
|--------|---------------------|-------------------|--------|
| Organic CTR | 1.76% | 0.61% | **-61%** |
| Paid CTR | 19.7% | 6.34% | **-68%** |
| Overall CTR | 15% | 8% | **-47%** |

Source: DataSlayer, Similarweb (2025)

**The implication:** Even when your content ranks in Google, AI Overviews synthesize answers without sending traffic. Your visibility in AI systems—not just search rankings—determines whether users find you.

### Publisher Traffic Collapse

| Publisher | Traffic Loss | Period |
|-----------|--------------|--------|
| Global publishers (average) | **-33%** | Nov 2024 - Nov 2025 |
| Business Insider | **-55%** | Apr 2022 - Apr 2025 |
| HuffPost | **-50%** | Same period |

Source: Press Gazette, SimilarWeb (2025)

**The reality:** Major publishers are losing half their search traffic. This isn't a temporary fluctuation—it's a structural shift in how information is consumed.
